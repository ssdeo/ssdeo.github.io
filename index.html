Introduction

Are you feeling down or anxious? Do you wish you had someone to talk to? Are you in need of help? Charlie the stress bot is here to help!

Who am I?

Hi! I am Charlie the Stressbot, your personal self-care assistant! I can help you manage your stress, recommend counseling, and answer any questions you may about mental health issues.

What Can I Do?

  • I can help you manage your stress by guiding you through a variety of de-stressing activities.
  • I can schedule your counseling session at the counselor’s earliest availability.
  • I can do a quick risk assessment to connect you with a crisis counselor immediately.
  • I can answer your questions about mental illness (Try it now! Just say “mental illness.”)
